6 Signals - sound, vibration, light

Portable radiation detector Radiacode-10X series is equipped with a combined interface for user interaction:

  • display;
  • buttons;
  • sound emitter;
  • LEDs;
  • vibration signaling device.
    Sound, light, vibration complement the information presented on the display and provide feedback when pressing the buttons.

In the menu you can select the operation mode of sound, light and vibration alarms. 

Separate permissions to play alarm sounds, buttons, clicks are available.
- Briefly press the up/down rocker buttons to move the focus to [Signals];
- Short press the round button to move the focus to [Signals].
- Briefly press the up/down swing buttons to move the focus to the item [Sound];
- Short presses on the round button to select the sound signaling mode:[On] - all allowed sounds;[Off] - only the sounds of searching for the instrument and going out of the measuring scale remain, if it is allowed by the settings.
- Vibration and light modes can be set in the same way:

- Move the focus to [Vibro] or [Light] by short presses on the up/down swing buttons;
- Short presses on the round button to select the [On] or [Off] mode.
- The presence of gamma and X-ray photon registration clicks can be controlled separately.
- By short presses on the up/down swing buttons move the focus to the item [Clicks];
- Short presses on the round button to select the [On] or [Off] mode.
- Briefly press the up/down swing buttons to move the focus to the [Buttons];
- Short press the up/down swing buttons to move the focus to [Buttons] or [Vibro];
- Short press the round button to select the desired response type.
- Separately, you can control the presence of the device connection and disconnection signal.

- Briefly press the up/down swing buttons to move the focus to [Connection];
- Short press the round button to select[On] or [Off].
- The presence of the on/off signal can be controlled separately.

- Short press the up/down swing buttons to move the focus to [Power];
- Short press the round button to select[On] or [Off].
- To set the alarms, move the focus to [Alarms];
- Press the round button briefly to confirm your selection.
- Short presses on the round button to select the alarm indication mode[One-shot] or [Continuous].
- To select an alarm channel, move the focus to the desired item, e.g. [Dose rate];
- Press the round button briefly to confirm your selection.
- Briefly press the up/down swing buttons to move the focus to the desired alarm level[Alarm 1] or [Alarm 2];
- Press the round button briefly to confirm your selection.
- Short press the up/down swing buttons to move the focus to [Sound] or [Vibration];
- Short press the round button to select[On] or [Off];
- To return to the previous level or exit the menu, briefly press the up/down swing buttons to move the focus to [< Back] or [<< Menu quit];
- Briefly press the round button to confirm your selection.
